BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN ENTREPRENEURSHIP

The BSEN program is an intensive exploration of entrepreneurial concepts, theories, and strategic frameworks. It cultivates innovation, creativity, and business acumen, empowering students to identify opportunities, mitigate risks, and drive business success.

The curriculum is based on the stantards set by the Commision on Higher Education (CHED) and is benchmarked from the curricula of leading academic institutions, national and international, offering similar business programs, and addresses the needs of the industry.

WHY BSEN COURSE?

Here are several compelling reasons why you should consider studying and pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Enrepreneurship.

Innovation

Fosters an environment that encourages innovation, creativity, and out-of-the-box thinking, qualities for entrepreneurial.

Business

Gain a deep understanding of business fundamentals, include market analysis, financial management, strategic planning.

Mindset

Entrepreneurial mindset, confidence, and resilience needed to identify opportunities, ideas into successful ventures.

Networking

Provide ample opportunities for networking with industry professionals, mentors, fostering potential partnerships.

Flexibility

Adapt to changing market conditions and emerging trends. Thrive in fast-paced and unpredictable environments.

Fulfillment

Impact and bring positive change to society through innovative solutions, products, and services.

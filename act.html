Associate in Computer Technology

The ACT program provides a thorough study of fundamental computing concepts, theories, and practical applications. It prepares students to become proficient IT professionals, researchers, and skilled developers of computing solutions.

The curriculum is based on the stantards set by the Commision on Higher Education (CHED) and is benchmarked from the curricula of leading academic institutions, national and international, offering similar business programs, and addresses the needs of the industry.

WHY ACT COURSE?

Here are several compelling reasons why you should consider studying and pursuing a Associate in Computer Technology.

High Demand

In-demand technical skills that are sought after across various industries, ensuring ample job opportunities upon graduation.

Versatility

Diverse career paths to choose from, including roles in software development, IT support, network admin, cybersecurity.

Advancements

ACT provides students with the knowledge and skills needed to adapt to new technologies and stay ahead in the field.

Time

ACT typically takes less time to complete, allowing students to enter the workforce sooner and start their careers.

Cost

Lower tuition costs compared to four-year degrees, making them a more affordable option for students in computer tech.

Pathway

Providing a solid foundation for transferring to a bachelor's program or pursuing specialized certifications in the field.

ACT

Associate in Computer Technology (ACT) is a smart choice for several reasons. This program provides valuable IT skills, offering hands-on learning for practical application, with a shorter time frame for completion.

Graduates can swiftly enter the workforce and explore diverse career paths in fields like software development and network administration. Whether aiming to jumpstart a career or pursue further education in the realm of technology.

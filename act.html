BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN ACCOUNTING INFORMATION SYSTEM

The BSAIS program focuses on fundamental concepts, algorithms, and the practical application of technology in accounting. Students become adept researchers, and proficient of computing solutions tailored for accounting needs for the modern accounting field.

The curriculum is based on the stantards set by the Commision on Higher Education (CHED) and is benchmarked from the curricula of leading academic institutions, national and international, offering similar business programs, and addresses the needs of the industry.

WHY BSIAS COURSE?

Here are several compelling reasons why you should consider studying and pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Accounting Information System.

Unique Skill

Accounting principles, equipping graduates with a distinctive skill set highly valued in the business world.

Specialized Career

Explore various specialized career paths such as Forensic ACCTG, IT Auditing, Compliance Analysis, and Financial Management.

Data Integrity

Maintaining integrity & security of financial data, compliance, regulations, and safeguarding against fraud and cyber threats.

Decision-Making

AIS empower organizations with accounting data and advanced analytics to make informed and strategic business decisions.

Connectivity

AIS facilitates communication and collaboration across international borders, supporting global business and financial.

Innovation

Evolving in technology and changes in regulatory, offering professional growth, and innovation in management.
